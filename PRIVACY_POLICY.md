# Privacy Policy for TikSave IO

**Last Updated: June 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **TikSave IO** ("the App") handles your information when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience.

## 1. Data Collection & Advertising Declarations

### 1.1 Personal Information & Advertising ID
* **No Personal Data Collection**: The App **does not collect, store, or share** any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account registration, login credentials, email addresses, or phone numbers.
* **No Advertising Identifiers**: The App **does not collect or use** the Android Advertising ID (AAID) or other advertising identifiers, as all advertising SDKs and advertisement functionalities have been completely disabled.

### 1.2 Non-Personal Data
The App processes basic technical data locally and transiently purely for core app functionality:
* **Device Information**: Model and OS version (standard Android framework queries for local configuration).
* **Network Actions**: Paste link queries to fetch video files.

### 1.3 Media Storage
All videos and photos downloaded through the App are stored **locally on your device's storage (Gallery)**. We do not upload your media or search queries to any cloud servers or external databases.

## 2. Permissions
To function correctly, the App requires:
* **Internet Access**: To fetch public video metadata and stream files during downloads.
* **Storage / Media Access**: To save downloaded files to your gallery (compatible with Android 13+ Photo/Video Media Store APIs).

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App runs without any analytics, telemetry, tracking, or advertising SDKs. The only active third-party interactions are:
* **Google Play Services**: Standard framework provided by Android for app distribution, updates, and integrity checks.
* **RapidAPI**: Used as a secure API bridge solely to retrieve public video metadata requested directly by you.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
The App does not collect any data. It complies with all standard Google Play Families policies. We do not knowingly target or collect data from children under 13.

## 5. User Responsibilities
*   Users are responsible for the content they download. Please respect the copyright and intellectual property rights of original content creators. **TikSave IO** is not responsible for any misuse of downloaded content.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
