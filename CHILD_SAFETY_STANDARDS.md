# Child Safety Standards: TTDownloader - TikTokio

**Published Date: April 17, 2026**

TTDownloader - TikTokio ("the App") is committed to ensuring a safe digital environment and maintaining the highest standards for the protection of children. These standards outline our zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

## 1. Zero-Tolerance Policy
The App strictly prohibits the use of its services for any activity related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). This includes, but is not limited to:
- Downloading, storing, or sharing Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
- Using the App to facilitate any form of child exploitation or harm.

## 2. Mandatory Reporting and Compliance
We comply with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including:
- **NCMEC Reporting**: In accordance with United States federal law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A), any actual knowledge of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) will be reported immediately to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).
- **Global Authorities**: We cooperate with international law enforcement agencies to combat child exploitation.

## 3. Removal of Content
While the App stores data only locally on a user's device, we explicitly forbid the use of our software for the processing of illegal content. If we become aware of any user using the App to facilitate CSAE, we will cooperate with legal authorities to ensure the termination of such activities and the removal of content where applicable under our control (e.g., promotional materials or user feedback).

## 4. In-App Reporting Mechanism
We provide a dedicated mechanism for users to report any concerns regarding child safety:
- **In-App**: Users can use the "Report Safety Concern" feature located in the App Settings.
- **Direct Email**: Reports can be sent directly to [shazikhantareen@gmail.com](mailto:shazikhantareen@gmail.com) with the subject line "CHILD SAFETY REPORT". All reports will be reviewed as a matter of top priority.
